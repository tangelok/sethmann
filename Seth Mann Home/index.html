Our Philosophy

Since 1921, the Seth Mann 2nd Home for Women has offered a unique alternative for women aged 62 and older who want to maintain an independent lifestyle, but need some assistance to do so. Our home offers long-term, short-term and respite care that is always provided with compassion and sensitivity.

The Seth Mann Home has accommodations for five women whose needs can be met in a residential care facility. Our dedicated caregivers assist each resident to ensure her social, emotional and physical needs are met. We maintain a home-like atmosphere while offering 24-hour care and amenities that are customized to each woman's schedule.

Our goal is to help every woman be as active and involved as she wants or is able to be. Our location is convenient to houses of worship, stores, restaurants and public transportation; residents are also able to keep their own cars if desired.

A Place to Call Home

The environment at the Seth Mann Home is inviting, supportive and nurturing. The services and amenities we provide our residents are all inclusive with the monthly fee and include:

  • A private bedroom for each resident
  • 3 home-cooked, well-balanced meals and snacks served daily and supervised by a registered dietician
  • 24-hour licensed/certified staff coverage
  • Exceptional staff to resident ratio -- a minimum of 1:5
  • Consultations with a licensed social worker available as needed
  • Medication supervision
  • An emergency call system
  • Assistance with personal care
  • Laundry services
  • Housekeeping services
  • Planned activities available

At the Seth Mann Home, we pride ourselves on the personal touches that make the Seth Mann Home feel like home to our residents.

  • Private, comfortable, spacious and bright bedrooms
  • Meals prepared to individual schedules and dietary needs
  • Living and dining areas to visit with and entertain family and friends
  • A renovated antique homestead filled with Mann family heirlooms
  • Peaceful and private outdoor spaces
